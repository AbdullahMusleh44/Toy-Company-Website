About Us

Amazing toys, Amazing prices.

The Northumbria Toy Company was established in 2003 out of Newcastle, England. With a vision of providing the best toys, in the cheapest possible prices to as many kids. After being loved and entrusted by families across Newcastle, Northumbria Toy Company has expanded to over 14 cities across the United Kingdom.

Commited to providing toys of golden quality, we have become the leading store for toys, action figures, learning games, dolls, video games and many more. Offering a wide range of toys to kids across ages, make sure to explore the latest toys at family-friendly prices only at Northumbria Toy Company.
